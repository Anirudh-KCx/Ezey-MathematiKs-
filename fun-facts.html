Fun & Surprising Math Facts

← Back to Home
  • Zero is the only number that can't be represented in Roman numerals.
  • A pizza that has radius "z" and height "a" has a volume of Pi × z × z × a (PIZZA!).
  • There are infinitely many prime numbers – proven by Euclid over 2,000 years ago!
  • 111,111,111 × 111,111,111 = 12,345,678,987,654,321
  • In a group of just 23 people, there's over a 50% chance that two people share a birthday. (Birthday Paradox)
  • The word “hundred” comes from the old Norse word *hundrath*, which actually means 120.
