History of Mathematics

← Back to Home

Math has evolved over thousands of years from simple counting to complex calculus.

  • Euclid – Father of Geometry (300 BC)
  • Archimedes – Pi and volume of spheres (287 BC)
  • Ramanujan – Self-taught genius from India (20th century)
  • Newton & Leibniz – Invented Calculus independently!
